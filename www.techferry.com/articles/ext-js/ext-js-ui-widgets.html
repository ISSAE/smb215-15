Ext JS - Rich UI Widgets


ExtJS is gaining traction recently as a client-side technology due to its rich UI widgets, Components and Layout support, MVC Architecture, Graphs and Charts. It is a cross bowser technology and save developers the hassles of testing on multiple browsers.
